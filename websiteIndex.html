We are so happy to finally release our first game UNIVENTURE! UNIVENTURE is "Univenture" is a hilarious and visually captivating adventure that puts you in the shoes of a university student on a mission to make it to class on time, no matter what obstacles stand in your way. In this comedic visual novel game, you'll navigate the chaos of a typical student's life. As you guide your character through a day filled with laughter and absurdity, your choices will shape the outcome of your university adventure. Will you brave the treacherous morning traffic, forge alliances with familiar characters, or even attempt unconventional shortcuts to reach those all-important lectures punctually? It's a race against time, where every decision you make adds a layer of hilarity to this unforgettable collegiate experience. Strap in for a rollercoaster ride of university antics as you embark on this comedic adventure, where the only rule is to arrive at the university on time, no matter how outrageous the journey may become.
