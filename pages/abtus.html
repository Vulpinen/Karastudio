Our Values

Inclusivity: We welcome individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels. Diversity fuels innovation, and we believe that anyone with a passion for game development should have the chance to participate.

Education: We are committed to teach one another and provide opportunities for members to expand their knowledge and improve their game development skills.

Collaboration: Games are the result of collaborative efforts, and we encourage members to work together, share ideas, and support one another's projects.

Passion: We are driven by our love for games and our desire to make a fun and enjoyable gaming experience for the players. Our passion fuels our determination to create amazing experiences.

Quality: Though most of us might still be beginners in programming or in other aspects of game development, we work hard and try our best to create good quality games that everyone could enjoy.

Exploring and discovering new things is an integral part of the fun and excitement in our circle. So, join us to connect with like-minded individuals, embark on exciting projects, and turn your game development dreams into reality.
Together, we are building a vibrant community that empowers aspiring game developers to reach new heights. Come be a part of our journey, and let's create games that inspire and entertain the world!
